Get answers to FAQs about our services

1. What services do you offer ?

At We Resume Brand Studio, we provide a comprehensive range of services designed to help you elevate your professional brand and enhance your job search. Our offerings include:

  • Resume Writing
  • Cover Letter Writing
  • LinkedIn Profile Optimization
  • Niche-Specific Resume Designs
  • Portfolio Development
2. Do you offer niche-specific resume designs ?

Yes, we do! At We Resume Brand Studio, we understand that each industry has its own standards, preferences, and expectations when it comes to resumes.
That’s why we specialize in creating niche-specific resume designs tailored to various fields, including: technology, Healthcare, Finance, Creative industries, Education etc.

3. What information do I need to provide for my resume ?

To create a tailored and effective resume, we need a variety of information that showcases your skills, experiences, and achievements. Here’s a list of the key details to provide:


  • Contact Information
  • Professional summary / Objective
  • Work experience
  • Educational background & Skills
  • Professional affiliations (if any)
  • Additional information (if required)
4. Can you help with cover letters and LinkedIn profiles ?

Absolutely! At We Resume Brand Studio, we understand the importance of a comprehensive job application, which is why we offer specialized services for both cover letters and LinkedIn profile optimization.
With our comprehensive services, you can present a cohesive and professional image across all your job application materials.
If you’re ready to enhance your cover letter and LinkedIn profile, simply reach out to us to get started!

5. How do I get started with your services ?

Getting started with our services at We Resume Brand Studio is simple and straightforward!
Begin by filling out our online contact form. This allows us to understand your needs, career goals, and the specific services you’re interested in.
After your consultation, we’ll ask you to provide details about your work history, skills, achievements, and any other relevant information.
Based on the information you provide, we’ll create a customized proposal outlining the services.
Once you’re satisfied with the final version, we’ll deliver your polished resume and any additional documents in your preferred PDF format
